TalkBlast

Mobile conference calling.

With TalkBlast you can create a conference call straight from your mobile phone.

  1. Sign in to your TalkBlast account in the Settings section
  2. Purchase some minutes if you already haven't done so
  3. Create an introductory recording for your attendees to hear in the Recordings section
  4. Finally, create a new or reuse an existing conference call in the Blasts section. You are ready to go!
